Animal Puzzle to Pass the Time!

This game is a mock puzzle game similuar to Puzzle and dragons. Though the method is much the same the creatures will not be, as these creatures will be a mix of dogs, cats, other normal animals and fantasy beings.

The main character in this game is going to be a creature of your choosing. the choices will be from there baby forms and only from either three animals at first. They will be a kitten, puppy or a bird. You will be able to gather more creatures through out the game, by gathering pionts to build trust in a wild creature. These beings will also be able to evolve.
